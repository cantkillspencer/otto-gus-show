// Who we are

Two brothers. One basement studio.

Two brothers sharing their journey through tech, gaming, robotics, and life as teenagers in 2026. Otto (13) loves robotics and computer science. Gus (10) is our gaming expert and creative director. Join us every week for honest conversations, cool projects, and way too many dad jokes.

// The story

How this whole thing started

It started with a single ring light, a borrowed USB mic, and a fight about which Mario Kart character is objectively the best (it's Yoshi).

What was supposed to be a one-time recording turned into a weekly habit. We started getting questions from friends, then their friends, then random people in Discord. So we kept going.

Now we're 42 episodes in. The setup is slightly more professional. The fights about Yoshi are exactly the same.

The show is for anyone who likes building things, playing things, breaking things, and laughing about it. Whether you're 9 or 39 — if that sounds fun, you're in.
